God Has Called Me to More

God has placed a purpose in my life that goes beyond the scoreboard. He has called me to play basketball with passion, discipline, and excellence, and to use that gift to serve Him with humility, love, and joy.

On the Court

When I step onto the court, I want to compete with heart and character. Basketball is more than a game to me; it is a place where I can grow in discipline, teamwork, and confidence while honoring God in everything I do.

To Serve Him

My calling is not just to play well, but to live well. I want my life to reflect Christ through my attitude, my actions, and the way I treat others. Whether I am training, leading, or encouraging someone else, I want to point people to Jesus.

My Mission

I want to use basketball as a platform to inspire, serve, and make an impact for God’s glory. Every practice, every game, and every challenge is part of the purpose He is shaping in me.

“Whatever you do, work at it with all your heart, as working for the Lord...”
